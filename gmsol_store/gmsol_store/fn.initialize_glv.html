ยงErrors

  • The authority must be a signer and have MARKET_KEEPER role in the store.
  • The store must be properly initialized.
  • The glv_token must be:
    • Uninitialized
    • Address must be PDA derived from GLV_TOKEN_SEED, store and index
  • The glv must be:
    • Uninitialized
    • Address must be PDA derived from the SEED of Glv and the address of the glv_token
  • The remaining required accounts are documented in InitializeGlv.
  • The length must be:
    • Greater than 0
    • Less than or equal to Glv::MAX_ALLOWED_NUMBER_OF_MARKETS
