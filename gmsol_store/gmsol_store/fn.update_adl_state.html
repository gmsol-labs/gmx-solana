Expand description

Update the ADL (Auto-Deleveraging) state for the market.

§Accounts

See the documentation for the accounts.

§Arguments

  • is_long: Whether to update the ADL state for the long (true) or short (false) side of the market.

§Errors

  • The authority must be a signer and have the ORDER_KEEPER role in the store.
  • The store must be an initialized Store account owned by the store program.
  • The oracle must be an initialized Oracle account that is owned by the store.
  • The market must be enabled and owned by the store.
  • Price feed accounts must be valid and provided in the market’s sorted token list order.
