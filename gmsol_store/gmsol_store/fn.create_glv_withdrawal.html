  • The owner must be a signer and have sufficient balance for the execution fee and rent.
  • The store must be properly initialized.
  • The market must be:

  • The glv must be:

  • The glv_withdrawal must be:
    • Uninitialized
    • A PDA derived from:

      • owner
      • nonce
  • Token requirements:
    • glv_token must be:
      • Properly initialized
      • The GLV token of the glv
    • market_token must be:
      • Properly initialized
      • The market token of the market
    • All other tokens must be properly initialized
  • Source requirements:
    • glv_token_source must be:
      • Properly initialized
      • A GLV token account
      • Have sufficient balance
      • Have the owner as its authority
  • Escrow requirements:
    • Must correspond to their respective tokens
    • Must be owned by the glv_withdrawal
  • All token programs must match their corresponding token accounts
