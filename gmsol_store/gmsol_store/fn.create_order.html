ยงErrors

This instruction will fail if:

  • The owner is not a signer or has insufficient balance for the execution fee and rent.
  • The store is not properly initialized.
  • The market is not initialized, is disabled, or not owned by the store.
  • The user is not initialized or does not correspond to the owner. The address must be a valid PDA derived from the owner and expected seeds.
  • The order is not uninitialized or the address is not a valid PDA derived from the owner, nonce and expected seeds.
  • For increase/decrease orders:
    • The position is missing, not validly initialized, or not owned by both the owner and store.
    • The long_token or short_token are missing, or do not match the those defined in the market.
    • The long_token_escrow or short_token_escrow are missing, not valid escrow accounts for long_token or short_token respectively, or not owned by the order.
  • For increase/swap orders:
    • The initial_collateral_token is missing or invalid.
    • The initial_collateral_token_escrow is missing, not a valid escrow account for initial_collateral_token, or not owned by the order.
    • The initial_collateral_token_source is missing or not a valid source account with owner as the authority.
  • For decrease/swap orders:
    • The final_output_token is invalid.
    • The final_output_token_escrow is missing, not a valid escrow account for final_output_token, or not owned by the order.
  • The feature for creating this kind of order is not enabled.
  • The remaining market accounts do not match the swap parameters, not all enabled or owned by the store.
