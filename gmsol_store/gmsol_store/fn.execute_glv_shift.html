  • The authority must be:

  • The store must be properly initialized
  • The token_map must be:
    • Properly initialized
    • Authorized by the store
  • The oracle must be:
    • Cleared

  • The glv must be:
    • Properly initialized

  • The from_market must be:

  • The to_market must be:
    • Enabled
    • Owned by the store
    • One of the markets in the glv
  • The glv_shift must be:
    • Properly initialized
    • Owned by the store
  • Token requirements:
    • from_market_token must be:
      • The market token of from_market
      • Recorded in the GLV shift
    • to_market_token must be:
      • The market token of to_market
      • Recorded in the GLV shift
  • Vault requirements:
    • from_market_token_glv_vault must be:
      • The escrow account for from_market_token in the GLV
      • Owned by the glv
    • to_market_token_glv_vault must be:
      • The escrow account for to_market_token in the GLV
      • Owned by the glv
    • from_market_token_vault must be:
      • The market vault for from_market_token
      • Owned by the store
  • Token programs must match the tokens and token accounts
  • The remaining accounts must be valid (see ExecuteGlvShift docs)
