Expand description

Execute GLV withdrawal.

§Accounts

See the documentation for the accounts.

§Arguments

  • execution_lamports: The execution fee claimed to be used by the keeper.
  • throw_on_execution_error: Whether to throw an error if the execution fails.

§Errors

  • The authority must be:
    • A signer
    • A ORDER_KEEPER in the store
  • The store must be properly initialized
  • The token_map must be:
    • Properly initialized
    • Authorized by the store
  • The oracle must be:
    • Cleared
    • Owned by the store
  • The glv must be:
    • Properly initialized
    • Owned by the store
    • The expected GLV of the withdrawal
  • The market must be:
    • Properly initialized
    • Owned by the store
    • The expected market of the withdrawal
    • Must be enabled and listed in the glv
  • The glv_withdrawal must be:
    • Properly initialized
    • Owned by the store
    • In pending state
  • Token requirements:
    • All tokens must be valid and recorded in the withdrawal
    • glv_token must be the GLV token of the GLV
    • market_token must be the market token of the market
  • Escrow requirements:
    • Escrow accounts must correspond to their tokens
    • Escrow accounts must be owned by the glv_withdrawal
    • Escrow accounts must be recorded in the glv_withdrawal
  • Vault requirements:
    • market_token_withdrawal_vault must be the market vault for market token, owned by the store
    • final_long_token_vault must be the market vault for final long token, owned by the store
    • final_short_token_vault must be the market vault for final short token, owned by the store
    • market_token_vault must be the GLV’s market token vault, owned by the glv
  • All token programs must match their corresponding token accounts
  • All remaining accounts must be valid per ExecuteGlvWithdrawal documentation
  • Returns error if execution fails and throw_on_execution_error is true
