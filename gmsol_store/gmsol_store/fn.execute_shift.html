  • execution_lamports: The execution fee in lamports claimed to be used by the keeper.

ยงErrors

  • The authority must be a signer and have the ORDER_KEEPER role in the store.
  • The store must be initialized.
  • The token_map must be initialized and authorized by the store.
  • The oracle must be initialized, cleared and store-owned.
  • The from_market must be initialized, enabled and store-owned. It must be the from market of the shift.
  • The to_market must be initialized, enabled and store-owned. It must be the to market of the shift.
  • The from_market must be shiftable to the to_market.
  • The shift must be initialized, store-owned and in the pending state.
  • The from_market_token must be the market token of the from_market.
  • The to_market_token must be the market token of the to_market.
  • The from_market_token_escrow must be a valid shift-owned escrow account for the from_market_token and recorded in the shift.
  • The to_market_token_escrow must be a valid shift-owned escrow account for the to_market_token and recorded in the shift.
  • The from_market_token_vault must be the market vault for the from_market_token and store-owned.
  • The feed accounts must be valid and provided in the same order as the unique sorted list of tokens in the from_market and to_market.
  • The oracle prices from the feed accounts must be complete and valid.
  • If throw_on_execution_error is true, returns an error if execution fails.
